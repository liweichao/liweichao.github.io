I am a research associate at Institute of Future Networks, SUSTech. I got my Ph.D. degree in March 2017 in Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Prof. Rocky K. C. Chang. I received my B.E. in Electronic Engineering and M.E. in Software Engineering from South China University of Technology.


Research interests:

My research mainly focuses on network measurement, especially on Internet measurement technologies, large-scale network performance monitoring and diagnosis, mobile network performance monitoring.

I'm also interested in areas including cloud computing, HTTP streaming system, quality of experience (QoE) measurement, and machine learning.


Leading or involved projects:
  • AcuteMon accurately measures the mobile network performance on smartphones (the source code will be released soon).
    •
  • MopEye measures the per-app network speed, and many more (capture packets, change DNS).
    •
  • Broadband Bauhinia measures the quality of residential networks in Hong Kong.
